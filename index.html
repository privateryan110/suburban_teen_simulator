Suburban Teen Simulator

This is a game where you play as a moody teen inside a house, doing whatever nefarious things moody teens do.

Controls:

-Move with WASD
-Grab items by clicking with your character's hand (grabable items will appear with a box around them)
-You can put items in your inventory by dragging them over your character and placing them in one of the boxes that will appear.
-Inventory is openable at any time using [i] or by clicking on your character
-Craft items by dragging them on top of each other (will create a new item if the combination is craftable)
-Use items by holding [shift] (some take a few seconds and may need to be held by your character's mouth to trigger their use
